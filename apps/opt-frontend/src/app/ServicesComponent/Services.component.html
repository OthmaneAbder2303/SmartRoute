Directions

The openrouteservice directions service covers the globe and allows you to compute routes and all sorts of navigation information. To this end, we offer a wide range of travelling options for multiple modes of transport. Amongst these you can choose between car, different bicycle types, walking, hiking, wheelchair, and heavy vehicle. Each of these modes uses a carefully compiled street network to suit the profile's requirements. To help you individualize your routes even further, the API offers the possibility to customize your input with road type restrictions and vehicle characteristics.

Long distance routes

With SmartRoute you are able to determine routes for car and heavy vehicle with distances within Marrakech with any kind of options added.

Can used by professionals

SmartRoute’s heavy vehicle routing option allows the planning of delivery for equipment and resources to disaster stricken areas, whilst taking into account restrictions based on vehicle properties.
